About KETA Kyung Nae

JOIN KEYSTONE
TAEKWONDO

At KEYSTONE TAEKWONDO ACADEMY, our mission is to provide high-quality Taekwondo training to students of all ages and skill levels. We believe in creating a positive and supportive environment that fosters personal growth and development.

Keystone Taekwondo Academy (KETA), established in 2024, stands as a beacon of martial arts mastery and family values. Located at 374 E Penn Dr, Enola, PA 17025, this academy is more than just a training center — it's a home for aspiring athletes and a nurturing environment for personal growth.

Meet The Team

OUR INSTRUCTORS

🥋
2nd Degree Black Belt · Chief Instructor
Instructor Ram Adhikari

RAM AD, second degree black belt, chief instructor has been practicing taekwondo for over 25 years. He owned and operated a taekwondo school in Nepal and is experienced in teaching ALL AGES athletes to their best — keeping in mind the world taekwondo policy and strictly following US SAFE SPORT RULES AND REGULATIONS.

👩‍🏫
1st Degree Black Belt · Instructor
Instructor Sonu Adhikari

SONU ADHIKARI, first degree black belt, has been actively practicing for at least over 2 decades. She is experienced in handling our LITTLE TIGERS & CORE TIGERS, bringing patience, enthusiasm, and expertise to our youngest students.
